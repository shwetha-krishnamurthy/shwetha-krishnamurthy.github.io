About

Background

I'm a Senior Product Manager at NVIDIA working on NIM with Dynamo — a platform for disaggregated LLM serving with KV cache-aware routing. I led this product 0-to-1: from customer discovery and UX design through co-architecting a Kubernetes operator with engineering, shipping Early Access in 6 months with 10+ enterprise partners on path to production.

Before joining NVIDIA, I spent four years at Goldman Sachs as a data scientist on Apple Card and GM Cards — building ML models that screened 60M+ applicants in under 10 seconds, and co-founding a cross-product customer knowledge graph platform that reduced organized fraud by 99% and saved $32M since MVP. I received the maximum performance rating for three consecutive years.

Earlier, at Yodlee, I built Seq2Seq NLP models for financial transaction categorization — 500× faster than the prior approach — serving 16K+ wealth advisors and 30M+ bank customers across North America.

Outside work: 14 years of Carnatic vocal training, 11 concerts performed, and a self-taught pianist.
